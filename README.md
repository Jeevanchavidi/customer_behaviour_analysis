# 📌 About the Project

Understanding customer behavior is one of the most important aspects of running a successful online business. In this project, I worked with an e-commerce dataset containing **3,900 customer records** to analyze purchasing patterns, customer preferences, product performance, and revenue trends.

The project follows a complete analytics workflow—from cleaning and preparing the data in Python, storing it in MySQL, answering business questions using SQL, and finally building an interactive dashboard in Power BI.

This project helped me strengthen my practical skills in data cleaning, SQL querying, data visualization, and business analysis.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

* Clean and prepare raw customer data
* Improve data quality by handling missing values and duplicates
* Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA)
* Load the cleaned dataset into MySQL
* Answer business questions using SQL
* Build an interactive Power BI dashboard
* Present insights that support business decisions

# 📈 Business Questions Solved Using SQL

Some of the business questions explored in this project include:

1. Which gender generated the highest revenue?
2. Which customers spent above the average while using discounts?
3. What are the top 5 highest-rated products?
4. How does spending differ between Standard and Express shipping?
5. Do subscribers spend more than non-subsscribers?
6. Which products receive the highest discounts?
7. How can customers be segmented based on spending?
8. Which products rank highest in each category?
9. Who are the repeat buyers?
10. How does revenue vary across different age groups?

# 📌 Key Findings

### Customer Insights

* Clothing generated the highest revenue among all categories.
* Young Adult customers contributed the largest share of sales.
* Subscribers spent more on average than non-subscribers.
* Customers with higher purchase frequency were more likely to subscribe.

### Product Insights

* Clothing was the top-performing product category.
* Products such as **Blouse**, **Jeans**, and **Handbag** received the highest customer ratings.
* Highly rated products generally showed stronger customer demand.

### Business Insights

* High-value customers frequently purchased products using discounts.
* Customer segmentation can help create targeted marketing campaigns.
* Interactive dashboards make it easier to monitor business performance and identify growth opportunities.

---

# 📌 Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations can help improve business performance:

* Invest more in high-performing product categories.
* Strengthen customer loyalty programs to encourage repeat purchases.
* Provide exclusive benefits for subscribers to improve retention.
* Promote highly rated products through marketing campaigns.
* Design personalized offers for young adult customers, who represent the largest customer segment.
